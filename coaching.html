Executive Coaching

Strengthening how you make decisions.

This is not about learning to lead. It is about how you think through and act on the decisions that matter most.

How this work is done

How this work is done

This is not advisory work or passive reflection.

I ask direct questions, challenge your assumptions, and stay with the decision until you are clear on how to move forward.

The focus is not on generating options. It is on making decisions.

The challenges I address

What brings leaders to this work

At the senior level, challenges are rarely about competence. They are about making decisions when the path forward is not fully clear.

i Making high-stakes decisions without full certainty
ii Navigating disagreement among highly accomplished peers and collaborators
iii Balancing mission priorities with operational and institutional realities
iv Communicating expectations and decisions clearly across diverse audiences
v Leading teams and peers through periods of change or uncertainty
vi Maintaining clarity of purpose amid competing institutional pressures
vii Remaining open to new information when leading from a position of authority
